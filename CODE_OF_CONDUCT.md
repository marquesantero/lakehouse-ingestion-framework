# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to a respectful, professional, and inclusive project environment. Contributors and maintainers are expected to collaborate constructively, focus on technical merit, and treat others with dignity.

## Expected Behavior

- Use clear and respectful language.
- Accept constructive feedback and provide it with concrete reasoning.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and the technical outcome.
- Be transparent about assumptions, risks, and limitations.
- Respect privacy and never share credentials, private data, or proprietary information without permission.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information or secrets.
- Deliberately disruptive comments, spam, or off-topic escalation.
- Bad-faith contributions that reduce project safety, security, or maintainability.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, commits, or pull requests that violate this code. Maintainers may also temporarily or permanently restrict participation when needed to protect the project and contributors.

## Reporting

Report concerns privately through the repository owner or GitHub's reporting tools. Security-sensitive reports should follow `SECURITY.md`.
